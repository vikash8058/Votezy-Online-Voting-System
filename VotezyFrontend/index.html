Help Center

  • How to register to vote?
    Visit the official Election Commission website or your nearest voter center. Fill out Form 6 and submit required documents.
  • How to check voter status?
    Go to the Voter Portal, enter your details, and check your enrollment status online.
  • How to vote online/offline?
    Offline: Visit your polling booth with your voter ID. Online: If eligible, log in to the e-voting portal and follow the instructions.
  • What documents are needed?
    Aadhaar card, passport, driving license, or any government-issued photo ID for verification.

About Us – Votezy

Votezy is a smart, secure, and user-friendly online voting platform designed to modernize and simplify the voting process. We ensure free, fair, and transparent elections using cutting-edge technologies like Java, Spring Boot, and MySQL, combined with Aadhaar-based OTP verification and optional biometric/face authentication.

👨‍💻 Developed By

Votezy is an innovation project by passionate students from Technocrats Group of Institutions, Bhopal, aiming to digitally empower India's democracy.

🎯 Our Mission

To bring technology, transparency, and trust to the heart of voting — ensuring every eligible citizen can vote securely from anywhere.

🚀 Future Goals

  • Integrate AI-based identity verification
  • Enable blockchain-backed voting for tamper-proof records
  • Launch Votezy for college elections, local bodies, and national-level pilots
